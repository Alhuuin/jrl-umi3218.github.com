Introduction

JANUS is a bi-located team that gathers expertise fromthe National Institute of Advanced Industrial Scienceand Technology (AIST), Japan and the French NationalCentre for Scientific Research (CNRS), France. Partic-ularly, it is formed by members of the CNRS-AIST JointRobotics Laboratory (JRL) in Tsukuba, Japan, and theInteractive Digital Human group at LIRMM in Mont-pellier, France. AIST is the co-developer with KawadaIndustries of the HRP humanoids and the main devel-oper of HRP-4C (Miim) and HRP-4 humanoid robots.CNRS possesses the only HRP-4 present in Europe.
