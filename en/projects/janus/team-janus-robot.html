Avatar robot

We are using a bipedal humanoid robot as our avatar. Among all the geminoids existing worldwide, HRP-4C[1] is the only one with close-to-human look that can effectively walk, manipulate objects, and realize facial expressions. Therefore, we didnâ€™t want to sacrifice the bipedal challenge with respect to efforts leaning moretoward well-known sensory feedback integration in anadvanced telepresence system. Instead, we rather aim at demonstrating an avatar in the shape of human that is (i) easily controllable, (ii) can provide rich sensory feedback even with a reduced master station set-up, and (iii) can be an elegant solution in many applications.
